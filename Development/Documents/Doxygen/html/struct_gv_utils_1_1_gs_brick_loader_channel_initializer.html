Detailed Description

template<typename TDataTypeList>
struct GvUtils::GsBrickLoaderChannelInitializer< TDataTypeList >

The GsBrickLoaderChannelInitializer struct provides a generalized functor to read a brick of voxels.

GsBrickLoaderChannelInitializer is used with GsDataLoader to read a brick from HOST.
